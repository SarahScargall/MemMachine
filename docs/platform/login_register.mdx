The User Guide - Login and Register page exists to provide helpful instruction on the multiple ways to login and or register to use the MemMachine Platform.

## Accessing and Registering to the MemMachine Platform

Access the [MemMachine Platform](http://console.memmachine.ai) via a web browser.  When you go to this URL, you will be presented with the following screen:
![Login Page](./images/login_page.png)

If you are a new user, you will need to register via one of the following methods:
<Tabs>
<Tab title="Username or Email" icon="user">
<Steps>
<Step title="Enter Username or Email">
Click on the blue "Register" link at the bottom of the screen:
![New User Register Link](./images/email-register.png)
</Step>
<Step title="Fill out Registration Information">
Fill out all required (*) lines of the Registration form:
![Login Password](./images/register.png)

Once you have completed the fields, click on the blue "Register" button to complete registration.  You will then be brought to the MemMachine Platform Console.
</Step>
</Steps>
</Tab>
<Tab title="GitHub" icon="github">
<Steps>
<Step title="Click on the 'Github' Button">
Click on the "Github" button in order to login with your Github login:
![Login via Github](./images/login-github.png)
</Step>
<Step title="Github Authorization">
You will be taken to a Github Authorization page asking for authorization to link to your Github account:
![Github Authorization Screen](./images/github-authorization.png)
</Step>
<Step title="Click the green 'Authorize memmachine-platform' button">
Click on the bright Green "Authorize memmachine-platform", which will bring you to the MemMachine Account Update Form:
![MemMachine Account Update Form](./images/mm-account-update.png)
This form pulls information from GitHub.  It asks that you also enter your Company and Profession.  
<Note>GitHub may also send you an email informing you of the request: "A third-party OAuth application (memmachine-platform-prod) with user:email scopes was recently authorized to access your account."</Note>
Once you complete this form, click on the "Submit" button, which will complete registration and bring you to the MemMachine Platform Console.
</Step>
</Steps>
</Tab>
<Tab title="Google Authorization" icon="google">
<Steps>
<Step title="Click on the Google Button">
To register via the Google Authorization method, click on the "Google" Button (highlighted in Red):
![Google Button on login screen](./images/login-google.png)
</Step>
<Step title="Chose Google Account">
Choose the Google Account you wish to use for logging in to memmachine:
![Google Account Login Page](./images/google-authorization.png)

Then click on that account.  Google will highlight the click by turning the background of the account a light blue.
</Step>
<Step title="Verify Google Authorization">
Google will bring you to a secondary Authorization Verification page:
![Google Authorization Verification Page](./images/google-verification.png)

If this matches the account you wish to register and login to MemMachine Platform with, click the "Continue" button.
</Step>
<Step title="MemMachine Update Account Information Page">
At this point, you will be brought back into the MemMachine platform, and asked to verify your account information, which completes the registration process:
![MemMachine Update Account Information Page](./images/mm-account-update.png)

Click on the "Submit" button when you have filled out the required (*) information, and registration is complete.
</Step>
</Steps>
</Tab>
</Tabs>
